Greevavasthy

Neck pain is a very common painful distressing condition of the modern day. Ayurveda offers the best remedies for neck pain and stiffness and related painful conditions.

Greevavasthy means a treatment in which medicated warm oils are poured and pooled for a fixed duration of time in a compartment or a cabin constructed over the neck or nape of the neck area using herbal paste.

This treatment is important to relieve neck pain as it provides significant and lasting relief from cervical spondylosis and whiplash sprains and strains; tension and headaches, migraines with aura, wry neck (torticollis), ankylosing spondylitis, osteoporosis, fibromyalgia; spinal stenosis
