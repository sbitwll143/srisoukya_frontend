What is Outpatient Consultation?

An outpatient consultation is a medical appointment that takes place in a medical facility, clinic, or hospital where the patient is not admitted as an inpatient. Outpatient consultations are typically scheduled appointments with a healthcare provider, such as a physician, specialist, or nurse practitioner, where the patient receives medical evaluation, diagnosis, and treatment recommendations.
