What is Classical ayurvedic medicine?

Classical Ayurvedic medicine refers to the traditional and ancient system of healthcare that originated in India thousands of years ago. It is a holistic approach to medicine that focuses on achieving balance and harmony in the body, mind, and spirit to promote overall health and well-being. In classical Ayurveda, it is believed that each individual is unique and possesses a specific constitution or dosha (Vata, Pitta, or Kapha) that influences their physical and mental characteristics.
