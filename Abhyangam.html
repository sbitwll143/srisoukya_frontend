Abhyangam

"Abhyanga" is a Sanskrit word that means the application of warm herbal oils all over the body with a gentle relaxation massage that is performed over a specially designed table by a trained masseur. Ayurveda recommends this as an integral part of the daily routine for all who wish for good health and longevity in life.

It is very relaxing and refreshing for the whole body and also takes care of the physical, mental, and emotional well-being of the person.

Benefits of Abhyanga therapy include:

  • Reduced muscle tension
  • Improved circulation
  • Stimulation of the lymphatic system
  • Reduction of stress hormones
  • Relaxation
  • Increased joint mobility and flexibility
  • Improved skin tone
  • Improved recovery of soft tissue injuries
  • Heightened mental alertness
  • Reduced anxiety and depression
