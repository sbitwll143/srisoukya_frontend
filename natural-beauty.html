What is Natural Beauty Products?

Natural beauty products refer to cosmetic and skincare products that are formulated with natural and organic ingredients derived from plants, minerals, and other natural sources. These products aim to minimize or eliminate the use of synthetic chemicals, artificial fragrances, and harsh ingredients commonly found in conventional beauty products.

Natural beauty products often emphasize the use of plant extracts, essential oils, herbal infusions, fruit and vegetable extracts, natural butters, and oils such as coconut oil, argan oil, jojoba oil, and shea butter. These ingredients are believed to offer various benefits for the skin, hair, and overall well-being.
