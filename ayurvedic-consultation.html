    • Ayurvedic Consultation

    An Ayurvedic consultation is a process of assessing an individual's physical, mental, and emotional health from an Ayurvedic perspective. It involves a thorough evaluation of an individual's physical constitution (prakriti) and imbalances (vikriti), lifestyle, diet, and mental and emotional state.

    The consultation is conducted by an Ayurvedic practitioner, who is trained to identify imbalances in the body and mind and recommend personalized treatment plans to restore balance.

    The goal of an Ayurvedic consultation is to help you achieve optimal health and well-being by restoring balance in your body and mind. The personalized treatment plan recommended by the practitioner is designed to address the root cause of any imbalances and promote long-term health and wellness.
